Market LOOSEMarket Structure: Derived from price action — TRENDING_UP (bullish trend), TRENDING_DOWN (bearish), or RANGING (consolidation). Sets the directional bias for dip classification confidence.
Growth EXPANSIONGrowth Regime: Based on NY Fed yield-curve recession probability. EXPANSION = normal growth. RECESSION_RISK = inverted curve signals elevated recession probability within 12 months.
Rates STABLERate Environment: From FRED H.15 Treasury yields. STABLE = normal conditions. RATE_SHOCK_UP = rapid rate increase (hurts REITs, utilities). RATE_SHOCK_DOWN = rate cuts (boosts rate-sensitive sectors).
FinCond LOOSEFinancial Conditions: From Chicago Fed NFCI/ANFCI index. LOOSE = easy credit, supportive for equities. TIGHTENING = credit contraction — increases risk for leveraged companies and suppresses valuations.
